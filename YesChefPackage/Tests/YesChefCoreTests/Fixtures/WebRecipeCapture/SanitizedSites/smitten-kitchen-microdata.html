Broccoli Cheddar Galette

A savory galette with a crisp crust.

By Fixture Cook

35 minutes 40 minutes Dinner, Baking

Ingredients

  • For the dough:
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 stick cold butter
  • For the filling:
  • 3 cups chopped broccoli
  • 1 cup grated cheddar

Make the dough

  1. Rub butter into flour.
  2. Chill the dough.

Bake

  1. Fill the galette.
  2. Bake until golden.
