Garlicky Spiced Chicken and Potato Traybake

Dinner

Total Time: 1 hr 10 min Servings: 4 servings Source: 177milkstreet.com

1/4 CUP EXTRA-VIRGIN OLIVE OIL

2 TEASPOONS POMEGRANATE MOLASSES

KOSHER SALT AND GROUND BLACK PEPPER

3 POUNDS BONE-IN CHICKEN PARTS

Toss chicken and potatoes with the spice mixture.

Roast until cooked through and serve with pan sauce.
