Kung Pao Chicken

Chinese, Cook's Illustrated

Cook Time: 40 minutes Servings: Serves 4 to 6 Difficulty: Medium Source: cooksillustrated.com

Sanitized real-shape Cooks Illustrated recipe.

CHICKEN

1 1/2 pounds boneless chicken thighs

2 tablespoons soy sauce

SAUCE

2 tablespoons black vinegar

1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil

Marinate the chicken.

Stir-fry with sauce until glossy.
